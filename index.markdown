## Roger Masclans

PhD Candidate in Strategy at Duke University, The Fuqua School of Business. 

### about
I explore the mechanisms driving the commercialization of scientific research. Some of my work employs advanced computational methods and innovative experimental approaches. 

Before joining academia, I founded a startup providing software and machine learning translation solutions for large firms. I also worked six years in M&A, participating in technological acquisitions in sectors such as advanced water treatment technologies, biotech, energy generation, and materials science.
