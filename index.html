PhD Candidate in Strategy at Duke University, The Fuqua School of Business.

about

I explore the mechanisms driving the translation of scientific discoveries into commercial applications and the diffusion of innovations into mass markets. At Duke, I'm a member of the Technological Opportunity Lab. Our mission is to provide research, novel data, and tools to support the commercialization of university science. Check out scientifiq.ai, our tool to help find commercializable science from around the world—and to advance research on innovation.

Prior to academia, I founded a startup providing software and machine learning translation solutions for large firms. I also spent six years working in M&A, where I led numerous international projects in technological sectors such as advanced water treatment technologies, biotech, energy generation, and materials science.
